About Course Vault

Course Vault is a free platform designed for students at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI). Our mission is to centralize tools that enhance your academic experience, including course navigation, a comprehensive backwork archive, and professor insights. Inspired by resources like QuACS and RateMyProfessor, we empower you to learn through real examples and historical backwork, helping you tackle challenging assignments and master advanced concepts on your own.

Our Features

Course Navigation

Easily browse and navigate the wide range of courses offered at RPI to plan your academic journey effectively.

Backwork Archive

Access past assignments and exams to understand problem-solving techniques and reinforce your learning.

Course Insights

Review detailed histories and ratings for prestigious courses to create your own experiences which best fit your learning style.

User Verification

Sign up using your school-issued email and RCSID to ensure a secure environment and contribute to our growing knowledge base.
